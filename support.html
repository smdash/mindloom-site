Common questions

The composer is greyed out / I can’t type anything.

Mindloom needs Apple Intelligence to turn brain‑dumps into plans. If Apple Intelligence isn’t enabled, the composer is intentionally disabled. To enable it: Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri → Apple Intelligence — on a supported device (iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 16, or newer).

How do I capture text from another app?

From Safari, Notes, Mail, or any app: select the text, tap Share, and choose Mindloom in the share sheet. The app opens with the captured text ready to plan.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your iPhone. Mindloom doesn’t use accounts, cloud sync, or analytics. Plans, tasks, transcripts — all on device.

How does Mindloom Pro work?

Pro is a one‑time purchase ($9.99) that unlocks unlimited plan history (free keeps your last three), custom templates, daily review, and automatic Reminders & Calendar scheduling. You can restore Pro on any iPhone signed into the same Apple ID via Settings → Mindloom Pro → Restore Purchases.

How do I delete all my data?

Open Mindloom → Settings → Danger zone → Delete all plans & reviews. This is irreversible.
